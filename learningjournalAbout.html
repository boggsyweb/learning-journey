About

Hi, I'm Stephanie.
Welcome to my learning journal.

This is where I will be posting some of the projects I make as I work my way through the Scrimba frontend career path program. You can follow along as I gain skills, make more complex and beautiful projects, and eventually get hired (eyes on the prize!)

Where I came from

In the fall of 2022, I found myself in a position where I needed to make a major career change. I toyed with a few ideas for a new path that but none really clicked, until one day I tried out a beginner course at FreeCodeCamp. The extent of my coding experience to that point was just a few HTML tweaks here and there on some ecommerce templates, so I wasn't sure if I could even get it. Much to my surprise, not only was able to learn, but I found I really loved it.

When it was time to get more serious about learning the skills that would eventually translate into a job, I searched high and low. I didn't want to go into debt to pay for a bootcamp, and the tutorials and YouTube videos weren't cutting it. That's when I discovered Scrimba!

My Journey

The instruction at Scrimba has made some of the concepts that I felt certain I could never grasp (looking at you Javascript) finally fall into place. Between the modules that break everything down into easy to digest pieces, and the support available on Discord, I am progressing faster than I thought possible. I'm happy to share what I am learning here.
