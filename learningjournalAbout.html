Hi, I'm Stephanie.
Welcome to my learning journal.

In the fall of 2022, I was faced with the difficult decision to change career paths from being a business owner to something new. What that new thing was, I had no idea. I toyed with a few ideas and eventually dipped my toe in the world of coding through an HTML/CSS course with FreeCodeCamp. It wasn't the easiest thing I had ever done by a longshot, but I was hooked as soon as I finished my first project.

Getting Serious

I knew if I wanted to get serious about coding, I had to take my learning to a level beyond the occasional tutorial. I completed a few courses on Courera. Some were great, and some were not quite what I needed. Then, I discovered Scrimba and things really started to click.

My Journey

You can follow my journey from panicking over what to do with my life, to diving into learning a new skill, and eventually getting hired (eyes on the prize!)
